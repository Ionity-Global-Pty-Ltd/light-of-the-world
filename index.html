A GENTLE RITUAL

Let every candle glow beautifully.

01 First light +

Let your first burn reach the edges of the vessel, usually two to three hours. This creates an even memory for every light to come.

02 Keep the wick kind +

Trim the wick to 6 mm before relighting. A short wick gives a calm flame, less soot, and a more generous fragrance.

03 Leave room for rest +

Allow the wax to cool completely between burns, and always keep a glowing candle within sight.
